About Me

Hi, I'm Truong Viet Hiep, a passionate photographer who believes that every moment is worth capturing.

My journey in photography began with a love for the art of visual storytelling. I lived in Tokyo for 3 years and fell in love with the visuals there. I bought my first camera, a Fujifilm XT30 with a 35mm f1.4 lens, to capture those moments. Now I'm back in Vietnam and continue my passion.

I specialize in beach, streetlife, food and portrait photography. And I want to share more about Vietnam through my photos.
